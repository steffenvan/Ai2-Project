<newSection> Abstract An overview of the SemEval-2 Japanese WSD task is presented.
It is a lexical sample task, and word senses are defined according to a Japanese dictionary, the Iwanami Kokugo Jiten.
This dictionary and a training corpus were distributed to participants.
The number of target words was 50, with 22 nouns, 23 verbs, and 5 adjectives.
Fifty instances of each target word were provided, consisting of a total of 2,500 instances for the evaluation.
Nine systems from four organizations participated in the task.
