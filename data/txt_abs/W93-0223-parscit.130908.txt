(AND AT LEAST TWO OPEN QUESTIONS) Ruslan Mitkov Machine Translation Unit University of Science Malaysia 11800 Penang, Malaysia Fax (60-4) 873335, Email ruslan@cs.usm.my My position paper addresses more or less Workshop question No. 5: &quot;How are rhetorical relations used in discourse understanding?
How are linguistic clues and world knowledge brought to bear?&quot; The paper shows how rhetorical relations could be used in Machine Translation (MT).
It introduces in brief, a discourse-oriented approach for MT which uses schemata of rhetorical predicates for describing the structure of a paragraph.
