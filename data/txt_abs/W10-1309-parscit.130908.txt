McCoy Interactive SIGHT is intended to provide people with visual impairments access to the kind of information graphics found in popular media.
It works as a browser extension, and is able to generate a summary of a simple bar chart containing its high-level intention as natural language text.
The user may request further information about the graphic through a follow-up question facility.
